About Me

Hello there! I'm Muhammed Nawaf, a passionate developer with a love for crafting beautiful and functional web experiences. My journey in the world of development began with HTML and CSS, where I learned the art of designing visually appealing and responsive websites. As I delved deeper into the realm of web development, I honed my skills in JavaScript, harnessing its power to create dynamic and interactive web applications.
